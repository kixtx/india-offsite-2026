📋 Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC)

⚠
Mandatory for all visitors to Thailand. Fill this out after booking your flight. Details must match your passport exactly. Download or print the confirmation to show at immigration.
  • Link tdac.immigration.go.th →
  • When to fill After booking your flight (use exact passport details)

FAQs

Common questions about the Bangkok offsite

Do I need a visa to enter Thailand?

Indian nationals can enter Thailand visa-free for up to 30 days under the 2024 policy update. Make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from April 27, 2026.

What is the TDAC form and do I need to fill it?

Yes — the Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) is mandatory for all visitors. Fill it at tdac.immigration.go.th after booking your flight. Details must match your passport exactly. Download or print your confirmation for arrival.

How do I book my flights?

Book your own outbound flights from any Indian city to Bangkok. Return flights are fully flexible — no specific city or date required. Feel free to plan onward travel after the offsite if you’d like! Aim to arrive in the afternoon on April 27 to check in before the welcome dinner.

How do I get from the airport to the hotel?

Please book your own transfer from the airport. Options include Grab (highly recommended), metered taxis, or prebooking a cab. You can use your monthly commuter budget for this. The journey from Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) to NH Bangkok Boulevard Sukhumvit is approximately 45 minutes.

What are the room arrangements?

Rooms are twin-sharing. Roommate preferences were collected in advance. Reach out to the People Ops team if you have any questions about your room assignment.

What is the weather like in Bangkok in April?

Very hot and humid — expect 33–38°C (91–100°F). Pack light, breathable clothing and sunscreen.

What currency should I carry?

Thai Baht (THB). Carry 4,000–5,000 THB in cash — exchange at the airport or arrange before travel. Credit cards are widely accepted at hotels, malls, and restaurants.

Will there be vegetarian / dietary options?

Thai cuisine accommodates a wide range of dietary preferences. It’s good to be aware of any food allergies you may have, especially when eating out during the city tour or at dinners.

Who do I contact for questions?

Reach out to the People Ops team — Kirti or Varsha for any questions about the offsite.
